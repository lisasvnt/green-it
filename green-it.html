What is Green IT?

Green IT (or sustainable IT) refers to environmentally responsible practices in the design, manufacturing, use, and disposal of technology. It aims to reduce the environmental footprint of digital technologies throughout their entire lifecycle.

From choosing energy-efficient devices to recycling e-waste properly, Green IT encompasses all efforts to make our digital world more sustainable.

Manufacturing: Producing one smartphone requires mining 70+ kg of raw materials, including rare earth metals extracted through environmentally damaging processes.

Energy Consumption: Digital technologies consume about 10% of global electricity, with this figure expected to rise significantly.

E-waste Crisis: Only 17% of e-waste is properly recycled. The rest ends up in landfills, releasing toxic chemicals into soil and water.

Carbon Footprint: The IT sector's carbon footprint is equivalent to the aviation industry and growing faster.
